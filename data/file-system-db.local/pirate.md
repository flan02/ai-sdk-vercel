# The Legend of Captain Iron-Eye

With a hook for a hand and a heart of gold,
Captain Iron-Eye, brave and bold.
He sailed the seven seas, wide and blue,
With a crew of scoundrels, a loyal few.

His ship, the 'Salty Dog', a sight to see,
With black sails flapping, wild and free.
He chased the horizon, the wind in his hair,
Searching for treasure, a fortune rare.

Through stormy waters and gales that blow,
He navigated currents, the tides' ebb and flow.
With a map in his hand, worn and old,
He sought the hidden secrets, the tales untold.

From the Caribbean shores to the Arctic ice,
He braved every danger, paid every price.
With a cutlass in hand and a roar of pride,
He fought every battle, with nowhere to hide.

But the treasure he sought wasn't silver or gold,
But the thrill of the chase, the stories told.
The wind in the sails, the spray of the sea,
The freedom of sailing, wild and free.

So here's to Captain Iron-Eye, the pirate king,
Whose tales of adventure, the sailors still sing.
A life of adventure, a heart full of fire,
A pirate's spirit, a timeless desire.
